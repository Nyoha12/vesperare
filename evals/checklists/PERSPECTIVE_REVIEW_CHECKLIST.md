# PERSPECTIVE_REVIEW_CHECKLIST

## Purpose

Use this checklist for board reviews, focus groups, subagent reviews, or multi-perspective critiques.

## Checklist

- [ ] The question or decision is clear.
- [ ] Each perspective has a defined lens.
- [ ] Perspectives are not collapsed too early.
- [ ] Disagreements are preserved.
- [ ] Confidence levels are stated.
- [ ] Simulated views are not treated as facts.
- [ ] Human decision remains explicit.
- [ ] Missing evidence is identified.
- [ ] Real-world validation needs are identified.
- [ ] The review produces a next action.

## Reject if

- all perspectives agree too easily;
- the output creates fake authority;
- the review hides uncertainty;
- simulated audience feedback is treated as real feedback;
- the AI makes the final judgment without human validation.

## Output

PASS / NEEDS REVISION / REJECT
